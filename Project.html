Why Mental Health Matters

Mental health affects how we think, feel, and act. Spreading awareness helps reduce stigma and encourage people to seek help without fear.

  • 🧠 Reduces stigma and shame
  • 🤝 Encourages seeking support
  • ❤️ Improves overall well-being

Simple Mental Health Tips

🌞 Get enough sunlight daily
🏃 Exercise regularly
💤 Maintain good sleep habits
📖 Practice mindfulness & meditation
